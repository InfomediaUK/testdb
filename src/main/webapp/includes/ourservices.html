Our Services

A robust procurement system

"Match My Job" (MMJ) is a vendor mutual procurement system that manages the temporary staffing supply to clients, giving a non biased opportunity for the client to choose the most appropriate temporary worker from quality assured Agencies.

MMJ will work in partnership with both Clients and Agencies to achieve the best possible practice available within the temporary staffing market. MMJ is a cost effective new generation technology providing a bespoke IT system that will save management time and money by reducing if not eliminating the problematic components surrounding agency bookings, such as sales calls, negotiations, timesheet management, invoicing, and all paper work processes, leaving a simple yet thorough transaction that allows the client and the agency a more effective way of running their businesses.

Support for in-house purchasing teams

Match My Job can advise and lead on the setting up of conditions of contracts through the tendering process, particularly for those agencies not regulated by a national framework agreement. As all temporary staff provided by agencies has to follow a strict registration and compliance process, to ensure consistent, high quality temporary staff and value for money for our clients. In addition all agencies are audited to ensure that they and their workers are fully compliant with the terms and conditions of each contract.
